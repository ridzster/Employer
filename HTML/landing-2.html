Common Questions

What is JobMatchingPTPTN?

JobMatchingPTPTN is a programme by PTPTN to fulfill the job demand of the industrial requirements of the companies to reduce unemployment.

Why is JobMatchingPTPTN different

a) Recruitment cost is sponsored by PTPTN
b) The successful candidates are trained with the industrial skillset required by the employer that meet the need of the job market
c) Provide the candiate profiling to the employers
d) TalentXchange will conduct 1st round of interview (Qualifying Candidates)
e) Screened and Shortlisted candidates are shared to the employers for the 2nd round of interview for hiring purposes

Is there fee that the employer need to pay?

No fees charged to the employer who has subsribed to JobMatchingPTPTN and TalentXchange.

What is TalentXchange?

TalentXchange is the talent partner of JobMatchingPTPTN programme with PTPTN. TalentXchange provides talent acquisition and placement services to the employers by identifying the potential candidates for the job placement.

What is the interview process?

The interview process is TalentXchange qualifying candidates and share to the potential clients based on the requirements of the companies for hiring purposes.
